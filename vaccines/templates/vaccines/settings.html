The app is in transition from legacy vaccine/group scheduling toward the new product/series policy model. Use the steps below when adding new logic.
1. Create concrete administered items in Products.
2. Create the clinical protection track in Series.
3. Link the allowed products to the series with priorities.
4. Add slot rules that define age windows, intervals, and recommended timing.
5. Use the legacy tabs only when you still need to maintain old schedule/group policy during the migration.
6. The next UI phase will add dependency and global-constraint authoring, such as "Pneumo after DTP + 15 days".
